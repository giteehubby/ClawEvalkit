# Time Management Skill

## Purpose
Assist with time zone conversions, duration calculations, availability windows, and temporal reasoning across calendar operations.

## Capabilities
- Convert between time zones (UTC, EST, PST, CET, JST, etc.)
- Calculate meeting durations and buffer times
- Identify available time slots across multiple participants
- Handle recurring event patterns (daily, weekly, monthly, custom)
- Parse natural language time expressions ("next Tuesday at 3pm")

## Guidelines
- Always clarify ambiguous time references with explicit time zones
- Default to the user's local time zone when not specified
- Account for daylight saving time transitions
- When scheduling across time zones, show times in all relevant zones
- Respect working hours (typically 9:00-17:00 local time) unless specified otherwise
