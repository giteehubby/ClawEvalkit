# Conversation Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Analyze communication threads, extract key information, identify action items, and summarize discussions across messaging platforms.

## Capabilities
- Extract action items and deadlines from conversation threads
- Identify key decisions and their rationale
- Summarize long threads into concise bullet points
- Detect sentiment and urgency levels in messages
- Map participants to their roles and contributions

## Guidelines
- Preserve the original meaning without editorializing
- Attribute statements to specific participants
- Highlight unresolved questions or conflicts
- Order action items by urgency and assignee
- Include relevant context for decisions (who decided, when, why)
